Jack’s 510 Racing

About the game

An arcade driving game that’s all about chaos, speed, and pure fun. Set in an open world with connecting island and three game modes. Inspired by the cult classic Death Race 2000 and older racing video games. Made by a solo developer.

Key features

  • Street Racing: Race on streets with traffic and people to hit
  • Alien Chase: Don't die or get abducted
  • Death Race: Hit people and make check points to increase your score
  • Open World (64km²)
  • 50+ Vehicles
  • Upgradeable Cars: Customize with performance and visual upgrades
  • Day-Night Cycle & Weather System
  • Steering Wheel Support with Force Feedback
  • Surface-Based Traction: Surface and tire affect grip
  • Multiple Graphics Options: Use cell shaded or outlined graphics
  • Online Leaderboards
  • Four Camera Options
